Titumir Hall

The boys' residence of Rangpur Cadet Coaching is "Titumir Hall". The hall is named after revolutionary leader Mir Nisar Ali Titumir. This hall is located in the Shaheed Muktar Elahi Chattar of Medical More in the traditional Rangpur city.

  • The hall consists of 170 seats in two floors.
  • Number of seats in a room varies on the size of the room. Usually there are two,three & fours seat rooms
  • The hall has a big hall room consisting big screen for the movie on Friday. Also there are opportunities for indoor games in the hall like caram, chess e,t,c,
